About Me

Turning ideas into
visual stories

Hi! I'm Esma Khanam, a passionate graphic designer based in Narsingdi, Dhaka, Bangladesh. I specialise in building strong visual identities — from logos and branding to print materials that make businesses unforgettable.

Every pixel I place is intentional. I believe great design isn't just decoration — it's communication. Whether you're a startup needing a fresh identity or an established brand ready to evolve, I bring creativity, precision, and a genuine love for the craft to every project.

I work with clients across Bangladesh and internationally through Fiverr and direct outreach, delivering polished, print-ready and web-ready assets on time.
